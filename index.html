My Projects

XelaBooks - Audiobook Player

A premium, user-centric audiobook player for Android, built for enthusiasts who want ultimate control over their personal library. XelaBooks provides a unified, modern interface to manage and play audiobooks from multiple sources.

Key Features:

  • Multi-Source Library: Import audiobooks directly from Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive, or local device folders.
  • Public Domain Access: Integrated search and download from the Internet Archive's massive LibriVox collection.
  • Metadata Enrichment: Automatically fetches high-quality cover art and book details from the Google Books and Open Library APIs.
  • Cloud Sync & Backup: Optional Firebase accounts allow users to back up and restore listening progress, library state, and bookmarks.
  • Full-Featured Player: Includes background playback, a persistent mini-player, advanced bookmarking with notes, and customizable themes.

Architecture & Tech Stack:

Built on a modern, scalable MVVM architecture with a focus on clean code and separation of concerns. The entire UI is developed with Jetpack Compose and Material 3 design principles.

Technologies: Kotlin, Hilt (DI), Coroutines, StateFlow, Room, Retrofit, Firebase (Auth & Firestore), ExoPlayer, Google Drive & OneDrive APIs.
